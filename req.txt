Patient Management System (Healthcare)
Objective: Create a secure portal for a medical clinic to track patient visits and histories.

Business Scenario: A local clinic needs to register new patients, look up medical histories during appointments, update contact information, and archive records for patients who haven't visited in 5 years.

Schema Fields:

patientId: String (Unique)

fullName: String

dob: Date

bloodGroup: String

emergencyContact: Number

lastVisitDate: Date

isChronicCondition: Boolean
